You open the doors to Amberial Dreams.
A cosmic toybox awaits.

A spherical character appears for you to control.
Seems like a platformer, but you cannot jump.

You begin to roll around a garden in space.
You feel the ramps and the hills, you start bouncing against the walls to gain height.

A portal appears, and you start your first challenge.
The world is dancing, little trinkets push you around, and you decide to join the performance.
Beyond, a universe of creativity, astral magic and floating factories lies in wait.

Features

  • Local Multiplayer (up to 8 people)
  • Create your own musical levels with an easy-to-use editor and music sequencer!
  • With only two buttons (left and right), Amberial Dreams is easy to play but hard to master
  • 100 Adventure levels
  • Co-op modes and other extras

This is the definitive entry in the Amberial series that started as a web game in 2007, attracting millions of players around the globe. Amberial Dreams brings together all the contraptions and concepts from previous games, and augments them with modern graphics, multiplayer, tons of content and new game modes!
